Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

My Easy List ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information:
When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we may receive your name and email address.

User Content:
Shopping lists, items, and shared list data created by you.

Device Information:
We collect minimal technical information required for app functionality, such as device type and operating system.

Voice Input:
Voice input is processed locally on your device. We do not store, record, or send voice data to any server.

Permissions:
The app may request access to your device microphone (RECORD_AUDIO) solely for enabling voice input functionality. Voice data is processed locally and is not transmitted.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the app
  • To sync your lists
  • To enable sharing between users
  • To improve performance and user experience

3. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase services. These services are governed by Google's privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

Your data is shared with other users only when you explicitly choose to share a list.

5. User Control

You can delete your lists or stop using the app at any time.

Removing the app may delete locally stored data, while cloud data remains associated with your account unless deleted.

6. Data Privacy and Access

Your shopping lists are private by default. Only you and users you explicitly invite can access shared lists.

We do not access your lists or personal content without your permission.

In rare cases, limited technical access may occur strictly for maintenance, security, or support purposes.

7. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on your consent and the necessity to provide the core functionality of the app.

8. Advertising

We may introduce advertisements in the future. If we do, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

9. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13.

10. Data Deletion

11. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any updates.
